The problem

Your Odoo data is one of your most valuable business assets, but getting quick answers from it safely is often difficult.

  • Business users need quick answers: sales this month, unpaid invoices, purchase orders, stock levels, and more.
  • Custom reports and exports take time.
  • Raw database access should not be casually shared with dashboards or AI tools.
  • AI answers need to be explainable and verifiable before users act on them.

The solution

Speak To Your Database Odoo Bridge is a small, security-focused Odoo module. It gives the Speak To Your Database service a controlled, read-only path into Odoo through the Odoo ORM.

  1. Install the bridge in your self-hosted Odoo 18.0 or Odoo.sh environment.
  2. Enable the bridge in Odoo and generate a bridge token.
  3. Connect Speak To Your Database using your Odoo URL and bridge token.
  4. Ask supported business questions and receive answers from your live Odoo data.

Bridge setup inside Odoo

Enable the bridge, choose the connector owner, generate a token, and save the connection setup directly from Odoo.

Connected in Speak To Your Database

After the bridge is enabled in Odoo, connect it in Speak To Your Database using the Odoo base URL and bridge token.

Security by design

  • Read-only access: The bridge uses the Odoo ORM for controlled reads, counts, and aggregations. It sends no SQL to Odoo.
  • No write path: No create, update, delete, or business-action execution.
  • No direct database credentials: Speak To Your Database connects through an Odoo bridge token เนโฌโ€ never your database password or raw database access.
  • Token-based authentication: Access uses a bearer token generated in Odoo settings. The token can be rotated or disabled. Use HTTPS in production.
  • Allow-listed models: Only a curated set of standard business models is exposed for read-only access.
  • Filtered fields: Sensitive credential, security, chatter, attachment, and configuration fields are filtered out and not exposed through the bridge.
  • Company-scoped reads: Access is scoped to the configured connector owner's Odoo company boundary.

The current bridge access model is company-scoped through the configured connector owner. Exact per-user Odoo record-rule parity is not guaranteed yet, and Speak To Your Database may further restrict access through workspace controls.

Installation for self-hosted Odoo and Odoo.sh

The Odoo Apps marketplace provides the module package. There is no one-click marketplace install: an Odoo administrator installs it like any other third-party Odoo module.

  1. Download the module package for Odoo 18.0.
  2. Copy the styd_odoo_bridge folder into your custom addons path.
  3. Restart Odoo.
  4. In Odoo, open Apps and click Update Apps List.
  5. Search for Speak To Your Database, then install or activate the bridge from the Odoo UI.
  6. Open the bridge connection setup page in Odoo.
  7. Enable the bridge, choose the connector owner, generate a token, and save.
  8. Connect the bridge in Speak To Your Database.
