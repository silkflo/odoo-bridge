The problem

Your Odoo data is one of your most valuable assets, but getting quick answers from it safely is hard.

  • Business users need answers quickly: sales this month, unpaid invoices, purchase orders, stock levels, and more.
  • Custom reports and exports take time.
  • Raw database access should not be casually shared with dashboards or AI tools.
  • AI answers need to be verifiable before users act on them.

The solution

Speak To Your Database Odoo Bridge is a small, security-focused Odoo module. It gives the Speak To Your Database service a controlled, read-only path into Odoo through the Odoo ORM.

  1. Install the bridge in your self-hosted Odoo 17.0 or Odoo.sh environment.
  2. Generate a bridge token in Odoo and connect it to Speak To Your Database.
  3. Ask supported business questions and receive answers from your live Odoo data.

Security by design

  • Read-only access: The bridge uses the Odoo ORM for controlled reads, counts, and aggregations. It sends no SQL to Odoo.
  • No write path: No create, update, delete, or business-action execution.
  • No direct database credentials: Speak To Your Database connects through an Odoo bridge token — never your database password or raw database access.
  • Token-based authentication: Access uses a bearer token you generate in Odoo settings and can rotate or disable at any time. Use HTTPS in production.
  • Allow-listed models: Only a curated set of standard business models is exposed for read-only access.
  • Filtered fields: Sensitive credential, security, chatter, attachment, and configuration fields are filtered out and not exposed through the bridge.
  • Company-scoped reads: Access is scoped to the connector owner's Odoo company boundary.

This early-access bridge is company-scoped through the configured connector owner. Full per-user Odoo record-rule parity is not applied yet, and Speak To Your Database can further restrict access through workspace controls.

Installation (self-hosted Odoo & Odoo.sh)

The Odoo Apps marketplace provides the module package. There is no one-click marketplace install: an Odoo administrator installs it like any other third-party Odoo module.

  1. Download the module package for Odoo 17.0.
  2. Copy the styd_odoo_bridge folder into your custom addons path.
  3. Restart Odoo.
  4. In Odoo, open Apps and click Update Apps List.
  5. Search for Speak To Your Database, then install / activate / upgrade from the Odoo UI.

After installing, open the bridge settings in Odoo, enable the bridge, choose the connector owner, generate a token, and connect it in Speak To Your Database. Odoo Online (odoo.com) is not supported, because custom server modules cannot be installed there.

What is not supported

  • Odoo Online hosted on odoo.com.
  • Write, update, delete, or business actions of any kind.
  • Raw SQL or free-form query building through the bridge.
  • Direct database credentials or database administration.
  • Full per-user Odoo record-rule enforcement (current early access is company-scoped).
  • General-knowledge questions not answerable from your Odoo data.
