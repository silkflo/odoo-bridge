Ask Odoo Business Questions. Get Answers You Can Verify.

Speak To Your Database Odoo Bridge connects self-hosted Odoo 19.0 and Odoo.sh to Speak To Your Database through a secure, read-only Odoo ORM bridge.

Ask plain-language business questions about customers, sales, products, stock, purchase orders, invoices, vendor bills, and CRM opportunities. Get answers from your live Odoo data with source, confidence, and clear calculation details.

Safe by design: read-only Odoo ORM access, no direct database credentials exposed, no write path, and unsupported questions fail closed instead of guessing.

Odoo 19.0 | Self-hosted / Odoo.sh | Read-only | External Speak To Your Database service required

The problem

Your Odoo data is one of your most valuable assets, but getting quick answers from it safely is hard.

  • Business users need answers quickly: sales this month, unpaid invoices, purchase orders, stock levels, opportunities, and more.
  • Custom reports and exports take time.
  • Raw database access should not be casually shared with dashboards or AI tools.
  • AI answers need to be verifiable before users act on them.

The solution

Speak To Your Database Odoo Bridge is a small security-focused Odoo module. It gives Speak To Your Database a controlled read-only path into Odoo through the Odoo ORM.

  1. Install the bridge in your self-hosted Odoo 19.0 or Odoo.sh environment.
  2. Generate a bridge token in Odoo and connect it to Speak To Your Database.
  3. Ask supported business questions and receive answers with source, confidence, and calculation details.

Installation (self-hosted Odoo & Odoo.sh)

The Odoo Apps marketplace provides the module package. There is no one-click marketplace install: an Odoo administrator installs it like any other third-party Odoo module.

  1. Download the module package for Odoo 19.0.
  2. Copy the styd_odoo_bridge module folder into your custom addons path.
  3. Restart Odoo.
  4. In Odoo, open Apps and click Update Apps List.
  5. Search for Speak To Your Database, then install / activate / upgrade from the Odoo UI.

After installing, open Connection Setup in Odoo, enable the bridge, choose the connector owner, generate a token (shown once), and connect it in Speak To Your Database. Odoo Online (odoo.com) is not supported, because custom server modules cannot be installed there.

Answers you can verify

  • Source on every answer: Answers show that they came from Odoo Bridge.
  • Confidence level: Deterministic answers include a confidence level.
  • How this answer was calculated: Users can review the Odoo model, record type, filters, grouping, limit, sort, and number of records matched.
  • Safe fallback: Unsupported questions return a clear unsupported message instead of guessing.

Security by design

  • Read-only ORM access: The bridge supports controlled reads, counts, and aggregations.
  • No write path: No create, update, delete, or business-action execution.
  • No direct database credentials exposed: Speak To Your Database connects through the Odoo bridge token, not by sharing raw database credentials with users.
  • Allow-listed models: Only selected standard business models are readable.
  • Allow-listed fields: Sensitive credential, security, chatter, attachment, and configuration fields are not exposed through the bridge.
  • Token shown once: The bridge token is displayed only once when generated.
  • Hash-at-rest in Odoo: Odoo stores only a SHA-256 hash of the bridge token.

Current bridge access model: sudo_company_scoped. Results are scoped to the connector owner's Odoo company boundary. Full per-user Odoo record-rule parity is not applied yet.

Version support

This Odoo Bridge module is currently validated for Odoo 19.0 self-hosted and Odoo.sh environments.

Odoo Online is not supported because custom server modules cannot be installed there.

Bridge support for older Odoo versions is planned after controlled validation. Until then, Speak To Your Database can also connect through a secured read-only database connection where database access is available.

Setup guide: speaktoyourdatabase.com/docs#odoo/bridge-module

What is not supported yet

  • Odoo Online hosted on odoo.com.
  • Odoo Bridge marketplace installation for versions older than 19.0.
  • Arbitrary custom models and custom-module discovery.
  • Non-allow-listed models beyond the supported models listed above.
  • Full accounting reports such as P&L, balance sheet, trial balance, general ledger, tax reports, and reconciliation.
  • CRM forecasting, lead scoring, marketing attribution, campaign ROI, email/chatter analysis, or private messages.
  • Projects, timesheets, manufacturing, maintenance, HR, fleet, helpdesk, subscriptions, rental, and POS modules.
  • Warehouse-specific analytics beyond safe internal-location stock checks.
  • Write, update, delete, or business actions of any kind.
  • Unrestricted SQL-style analytics or free-form query building through the bridge.
  • General-knowledge questions not answerable from your Odoo data.
  • Full per-user record-rule enforcement.

Validated in a controlled sandbox
